Refund Policy

Clarity on cancellations, credits, and refunds for Wissen Ecom subscriptions and services.

Summary: We offer a straightforward refund window for new subscriptions and address billing issues promptly.

Last updated: 12 April 2024

Refund Policy details

  1. Subscription trials & first-time purchases

    New customers receive a complimentary onboarding period. If you decide not to continue within 14 days of your first paid invoice, submit the support request form (choose "Billing & subscriptions") and we will process a full refund for the latest subscription charge.

    Refunds are not available for subscription renewals that occur after the initial term. We send renewal reminders so you can adjust your plan ahead of the next billing cycle.

  2. Eligibility criteria

    • The request must be raised from the account owner or authorised billing contact.
    • The request must be submitted within 14 days of the invoice date for first-time payments.
    • No refunds are provided when terms of service, data usage policies, or fair usage limits are violated.

  3. How to request a refund

    • Open the support request form and choose "Billing & subscriptions" with the subject line "Refund Request".
    • Include your registered email, invoice number, billing amount, and reason for cancellation.
    • Our finance desk will acknowledge within 2 business days and may request supporting information to verify the account.
    • Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method. Depending on your bank, credit to your account can take 5-7 business days.

  4. Services not eligible for refund

    • Implementation, onboarding, custom development, or consulting fees when work has already started.
    • Monthly or annual subscription renewals after the standard evaluation window.
    • Marketplace fees, government taxes, or third-party charges passed through the platform.

  5. Plan upgrades, downgrades, and credits

    Upgrading mid-cycle triggers a prorated charge for the remaining period. When you downgrade, the new rate becomes effective from the next billing cycle—no partial refunds apply for the current period.

    If we issue promotional credits or goodwill adjustments, they are non-transferable and expire on the date mentioned in the credit memo.

  6. Payment delays & charge disputes

    If a charge fails, we will notify you and retry up to three times before suspending the account. To avoid service interruptions, keep your payment method current.

    For chargebacks raised via your bank or card network, we reserve the right to suspend access while the dispute is investigated. We may request supporting documentation to resolve the claim.

  7. Contact

    Questions about billing or this Refund Policy? Submit the support request form (select "Billing & subscriptions") and we will respond within one business day.
