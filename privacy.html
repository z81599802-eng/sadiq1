Privacy Policy

Your trust matters to us. This policy explains how Wissen Ecom collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: 12 April 2024

Privacy Policy details

  1. Who we are

    Wissen Ecom ("Company", "we", "our", or "us") operates a software platform that helps ecommerce sellers automate reconciliation, inventory, and analytics workflows. This Privacy Policy applies to our website, web application, APIs, email communications, and any related services (collectively, the "Services").

  2. Information we collect

    We collect information in three primary ways: when you provide it directly, when it is automatically captured while you interact with the Services, and when third parties share it with us.

    • Account & profile data: name, business name, email, phone number, billing address, time zone, and other onboarding details.
    • Commerce & financial data: marketplace order information, settlement files, catalog records, inventory metrics, advertising performance, payment confirmation numbers, and GST/TDS identifiers you decide to sync.
    • Usage & device data: log files, IP address, browser type, pages viewed, session duration, and cookies that help us secure accounts and improve the experience.
    • Support interactions: survey responses, service tickets, chat transcripts, and call recordings captured with your consent.

  3. How we use your information

    We process personal information for legitimate business purposes, including:

    • Providing, personalising, and maintaining the Services you subscribe to.
    • Reconciling orders, payments, and returns as requested by you or your authorised teammates.
    • Improving product performance, building new features, and conducting analytics that guide roadmap decisions.
    • Communicating with you about account activity, feature updates, security alerts, invoices, and marketing tips (you can opt out of non-essential messages at any time).
    • Protecting the platform, preventing fraud, enforcing agreements, and complying with legal obligations.

  4. Sharing & disclosure

    We never sell personal data. We may share information with carefully vetted partners in these scenarios:

    • Service providers that help us host infrastructure, process payments, deliver emails, provide analytics, or offer customer success support.
    • Marketplace or logistics partners when you connect their integrations to your Wissen Ecom account.
    • Professional advisers, auditors, or regulators when required by law or to protect our legal rights.
    • With your consent, or at your direction, including when you invite collaborators or enable third-party apps.

  5. Cookies & tracking

    We use cookies, tags, and similar technologies to remember your preferences, keep you signed in, and measure how features perform. You may control cookies through your browser settings; however, disabling essential cookies could impact core functionality.

  6. Data retention

    We retain personal information for as long as it is necessary to deliver the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When data is no longer required, we follow industry-standard procedures to delete or anonymise it.

  7. Security practices

    We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect data, including encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access controls, periodic vulnerability reviews, and staff training. While we work hard to safeguard your information, no system is completely secure—please use unique, strong passwords and enable multi-factor authentication where available.

  8. Your rights & choices

    Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access, correction, deletion, or a copy of your personal data. You can also object to processing or withdraw consent for specific activities. Submit requests via the contact information provided below, and we will respond within applicable timelines.

  9. Third-party links & services

    Our Services may contain links to external sites or integrations we do not control. This Privacy Policy does not cover the practices of those third parties. Please review their privacy statements before sharing your information.

  10. Updates to this policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced via email or prominent notice within the product. Continued use of the Services after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

  11. Contact us

    If you have questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, submit the support request form or write to: Wissen Ecom, Compliance Desk, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560001, India.
